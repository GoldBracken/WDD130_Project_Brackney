Preparing for Your First Interview

Interviews allow employers to learn about you and your skills. Preperation will help you fell more confident

Common Interview Questions:

Tell me about yourself.

Tip: Talk briefly about your education, interests, and career goals.

Why do you want this job?

Tip: Explain what interests you about the company and role

What are your strengths?

Examples:

  • Good communication
  • Strong work ethic
  • Problem-solving

Interview Tips:

  • Research the company
  • Practice answering questions
  • Dress professionally
  • Arrive early
  • Bring a copy of your resume

After the Interview

Send a short thank-you email to show appreciationfo

Example: Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I enjoyed learning more about the role and your team. I look forward to hearing from you
