Finding Your First Job or Internship

Interviews allow employers to learn about you and your skills. Preparation will help you feel more confident.

Where to Look

Online Job Boards

  • LinkedIn
  • Indeed
  • Handshake

Company Websites

  1. Find companies you're interested in
  2. Visit their website
  3. Look for the Careers section

Career Centers (if you are a student) can help:

  • Review resumes
  • Practice interviews
  • Find internships
